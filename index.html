Why would you invest your time to learn about AI?

Artificial Intelligence is a powerful force changing the way we live our lives.

We humans are at the helm of this force, however it should be shared for the good of the planet, not only in the hands of a select few.

As Artificial Intelligence is integrated into more parts of our lives, this inequality looks to increase.

This increased integration is already showing, humans are relying more and more on AI to make our decisions for us.

For example:

  • Netflix, Spotify, Youtube, & Instagram recommending you content rather than you deciding for yourself.
  • The top chess players are recommended moves by Chess AI's
  • Warehouse's optimize the movement of human workers to maximise efficiency
  • Purchase recommendation's in the form of optimized advertising run deeply throughout all areas of life
  • etc..

That is to say, humans are currently embracing the "robot takeover" with open arms.

While we transition into the age of AI, we need to ensure our individual and collective actions propel humanity towards a future with equal oppourtunity to AI's benefits.

And so our mission statement, basic artifical intelligence literacy for all.
