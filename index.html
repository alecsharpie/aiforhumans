Basic Artificial Intelligence Literacy

Focusing on interpretting AI models to an ELI5 level

Artificial Intelligence is a powerful force changing the way we live our lives.

We humans are at the helm of this force.

Lets take this oppourtunity to learn from these models, like the powerful teachers they are.

Doesn't that sound better than guiding human attention towards ads?

After all, didn't we create the training sets they learned from?

How do AI make their decisions?

The fundamental simailarity is we are relying more and more on AI to make our decisions for us.

"Robots" already control alot of what we do

We are, and will continue to, embrace the "robot takeover"

For some, this heavy reliance on a mysterious force will be frightening

And so I revert back to our mission statement

Everybody should have Basic AI Literacy, to help soften the blow, and ease the transition into the age fo AI.
